# Mumega Product Vision

**Date:** 2026-04-08
**Author:** Hadi + Kasra

---

## What Mumega Is

Mumega is an AI that operates businesses. Not a platform to sell. Not a marketplace for agents.

Give it a domain + niche. It builds the site, writes the content, drives the traffic, learns what works, and gets smarter monthly. The organism.

DNU, TROP, GAF, AgentLink -- these are not 24 separate products. They are businesses the organism operates.

## The Moat: Mirror + Gemma

- **Mirror** = long-term memory (Supabase pgvector, semantic search, A+ priority)
- **Gemma 4** = trainable brain (free, per-tenant fine-tuning, compounds monthly)

Every business the organism operates generates data. That data trains the model. The model gets better at operating that specific business. This compounds. Competitors would need to replicate months of accumulated learning per tenant. That is the moat.

Never deprioritize Mirror. It is the moat.

## The Hands: SitePilotAI

239 MCP tools. WordPress powers 43% of the web. SitePilotAI gives the organism hands to build, edit, publish, and manage WordPress sites autonomously.

Combined with LangGraph, CrewAI, and ToRivers adapters, the organism can use any agent framework to execute.

## Revenue Model

Operate businesses directly. Not sell a platform.

| Tier | Price | What |
|------|-------|------|
| Operate | $2,500-5,000/mo | Full AI business operation (SEO, content, leads, dev, ops) |
| Activate | $1,000-2,000/mo | Specific capability (SEO-only, content-only) |

**Target market:** Mid-market businesses with real budgets. PECB, Viamar, C21 Barrie. Not $299/mo WordPress clients.

90%+ margin on routine work (SEO, content, outreach -- runs on free models).
70%+ margin on dev work (Haiku/Sonnet, not Opus).

Marketplace for ex-CDAP advisors and digital consultants. They plug in their expertise as skills. The organism executes. They earn per-result. Turns a network of freelancers into a scalable delivery engine.

# Business Model

**Updated:** 2026-04-15

---

## Two Products

### 1. Inkwell (Free Fork)

Open-source Astro content framework. Anyone can fork and deploy for free.

- **Purpose:** Distribution. Put Mumega tools in the hands of developers.
- **Distribution:** Listed on MCP marketplaces (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, OpenClaw)
- **Conversion path:** Fork → use free → hit limits → upgrade to Mumega SaaS

### 2. Mumega SaaS (MCP Connection)

One config line connects any AI to a business operating system: memory, tasks, publishing, payments, marketplace.

| Plan | Price | Seats | Key Features |
|------|-------|-------|-------------|
| Starter | $29/mo | 1 | Memory, tasks, basic publishing |
| Growth | $79/mo | 5 | + Glass Commerce, advanced publishing |
| Scale | $199/mo | Unlimited | + Full marketplace access, custom domain |

## Revenue Streams

| Stream | Source | Model |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Subscriptions | SaaS tenants | Recurring monthly |
| Glass Commerce | 5% of payment volume through Inkwell Worker | Transaction fee |
| Marketplace commissions | 5% of seller MRR | Platform fee |
| Full operation | Viamar-style clients ($2,500-5,000/mo) | Service retainer |

## Distribution: MCP Marketplace

Customers discover Mumega via listings on:
- Claude App Store / MCP directory
- ChatGPT plugin marketplace
- Cursor plugin directory
- OpenClaw marketplace

Each listing surfaces the 13 customer tools. Students and indie hackers start free (Inkwell fork); professionals pay $29-199/mo for Mumega SaaS; enterprises get custom operations.

## Customer Journey

```
Student / developer
    → Forks Inkwell (free)
    → Deploys personal site
    → Connects to Mumega SaaS (Starter $29)
    → Business starts growing, needs more seats (Growth $79)
    → Needs full operation (Scale $199 or custom)
    → Refers other businesses → marketplace listings
```

## Margin Profile

- 90%+ on routine AI work (SEO, content, outreach — runs on free Gemma/Haiku)
- 70%+ on dev work (Haiku/Sonnet, not Opus)
- Near-zero marginal cost to add a tenant at Starter tier

## The Moat

Mirror (semantic memory) + Gemma fine-tuning per tenant. Every business operated generates data. Data trains the model. The model gets better at operating that specific business. Compounds monthly. Replicating this requires months of accumulated learning per tenant.

## ToRivers Layer

Marketplace for ex-CDAP advisors and digital consultants. They publish squads as listings. Mumega executes. They earn per subscriber minus 5% platform fee. Turns a network of freelancers into a scalable delivery engine without headcount.
